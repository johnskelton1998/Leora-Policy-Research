- Family health insurance premiums averaged $26,993 in 2025, up 6% ($1,408) from 2024 [13]

- Increased demand for costly treatments (GLP-1 drugs, specialty care) [16]

**Impact on Home Health Agencies:**
Home healthcare agencies face dual pressure:
1. Rising workers compensation costs due to high injury rates in patient handling
2. Increasing professional liability premiums as claim sizes grow
